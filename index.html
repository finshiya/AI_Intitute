About QBix AI Coding Labs

QBix AiCodingLabs, a division of Insight Technology, is a next-generation learning and innovation hub focused on building AI-driven digital intelligence for the modern world. We specialize in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, Full-Stack Development, and Digital Media Creations — empowering learners to turn ideas into intelligent, scalable, and creative solutions.

At QBix AiCodingLabs, we go beyond theory. Every learner experiences a hands-on, project-oriented approach, combining coding, analytics, and real-world application development with creative digital production. Our unique integration of AI and digital media helps students explore how intelligence can shape content creation, automation, storytelling, and next-generation user experiences.

What We Do

  • • Train and mentor students in AI, ML, and Data Science technologies
  • • Build solid foundations in Full-Stack Web and App Development (Python, Django, MERN)
  • • Integrate Digital Media Creations and AI for smarter content workflows
  • • Develop industry-ready projects that combine technology and creativity
  • • Offer internships and real project exposure under Insight Technology's ecosystem
